Summary

Inception is a sci-fi thriller that follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in extracting secrets from people's subconscious during dreams. He is offered a chance to have his criminal record erased if he can successfully perform an impossible task—planting an idea in someone's mind.
