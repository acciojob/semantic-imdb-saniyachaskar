Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a thief who specializes in infiltrating the subconscious of his targets to steal valuable information. Given a chance to erase his past crimes, Cobb must achieve the impossible task of planting an idea in someone’s mind — a process known as inception.

Actors

The film features a brilliant cast including:

  • Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb
  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
  • Ken Watanabe as Saito
